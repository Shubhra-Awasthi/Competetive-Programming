1. Modulo Arithmetic:
    (a+b) mod m = ((a mod m) + (b mod m)) mod m
    (a*b) mod m = ((a mod m) * (b mod m)) mod m
    (a-b) mod m = ((a mod m) - (b mod m) + m) mod m
    (a/b) mod m = (a * b^-1) mod m  -->  where b^{-1} is the modular multiplicative inverse of b modulo m.

3. Given Prime factorization, Find no. of divisors of N:
	N = P1^{y1} * P2^{y2} * P3^{y3} * ... * Pn^{yn}
	
	Total divisors = (y1 + 1) * (y2 + 1) * (y3 + 1) * ... * (yn + 1)
	Where {P} are prime factors and {yi} are their powers (exponents).

5. Number of Common Divisors of N and K:
	a) Find gcd(N, K): Calculate the Greatest Common Divisor (G) of N and K:
		G = gcd(N, K)
	b) Factorize G: Find the prime factorization of G:
		G = P1^{g1} * P2^{g2} * ... * Pn^{gn}
	c) Count Divisors: Apply the standard divisor counting formula to G. The number of common divisors is the total number of divisors of G.
		Count = (g1 + 1) * (g2 + 1) * ... * (gn + 1)


6) Stars and Bars Problem:
	General Solution: A1 + A2 + A3 + ... + Ak = N
	Stars = N 
	Bars = K - 1

	a) Number of Non-Negative Solutions of Equation:
		Total Integer Solution = C(N+K-1, K-1) or C(N+K-1, N)
	
	b) Number of Non-Negative Non-Zero Solutions of Equation:
		General Solution = C(N-1, K-1)
